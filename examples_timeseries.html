Scale-space decompositionbla

Decomposition of a sensor signal into components for events occurring on different time scales. Detecting transient events when these events occur on different timescales in data prone to baseline shifts can be very tricky. Through scale-space decomposition, we decompose the raw signal into its natural components, and use those to detect events.
